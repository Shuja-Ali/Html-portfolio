The Best Movies According to Shuja Ali

My top 5 movies of all-time

1. No Country For Old Men

Love this gripping neo-western thriller exploring the consequences of a botched drug deal in Texas.

2. Fight Club

A dark psychological drama about identity, consumerism, and an underground fight club that spirals into chaos

3. Identity

Wonderful psychological thriller where ten strangers stranded at a motel are mysteriously killed off one by one, revealing a deeper connection between them.

4. Fargo

A darkly comedic crime thriller about a botched kidnapping in rural Minnesota that spirals into violence and chaos.

5. Primal Fear

A gripping legal thriller about a defense attorney who uncovers shocking truths while defending an altar boy accused of murder.
